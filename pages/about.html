FAQ

How did you get in to the sport ?

My love for contact sport and the desire to become a champion. I joined MMA hoping to find my place among fighters and to learn, however it didn't fill my cup and started looking for other opportunities that fits my fighting style. With my family's motivation and support I've finally found my flighting foundation.


What is your favourite strike to use in a fight ?

My favourite strike is the jab. It's deceiving and allows me to simultaneously use offence and defence measures and create openings to the head and body.


Who is your biggest inspiration ?

Mike Tyson is definitely my inspiration because of how determined he was to be the best and the type of fighter he is. He is an roll model to a lot of young boxers and he inspired me to become a boxer.


What team are you training with?

The best club in the southern part of Johannesburg, South-hills Boxing Club and with the best team that motivates each other, trained by our coach Lyle. It's the best place to be and my second home. What does it mean for you to be a boxer? It means everything to me, there is nothing i'd rather be than a professional boxer. It's like reading my Bible, it gives me motivation and stength and a clear mind. I never walk out of a training session regretting what I've leaned.
